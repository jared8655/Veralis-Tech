Veralis Tech is a Minecraft modpack focused on optimization and performance, specifically designed to adapt to different technical needs within the game.

Its main objective is to provide a more stable and efficient experience by reducing unnecessary system load and improving overall performance, making it suitable for both low-end devices and high-end setups.

This modpack is intended for various playstyles, including traditional survival worlds as well as more demanding modes such as hardcore, where stability and consistent performance are essential. Through its technical approach, Veralis Tech aims to maintain smooth gameplay without compromising the original Minecraft experience, instead enhancing it through carefully selected optimizations.
